## Phase 1: Strategic Vision

### The Value Proposition of Medya

> Medya is a sophisticated, user-centric multimedia ecosystem designed to empower our community.

## Core Rationale

### 1. Target Audience

- **Knowledge Seekers:** Discerning users engaged in programming, philosophy, the arts, and related intellectual discourse.
- **Existing User Base:** Specifically tailored for the _tewn.medya_ community.
- **Future Segments:** Scaling as required (Ongoing updates).

### 2. Problem Statement & Solutions

- **Decentralization & Autonomy:** Offering a platform independent of centralized government infrastructure.
- **Resilient Connectivity:** Ensuring seamless access to content within restricted network environments without requiring international internet connectivity.
- **Unified Experience:** Migrating existing Telegram-based workflows to a native web environment.
- **Censorship Resistance:** Providing a secure, VPN-free experience.
- **Data Integrity:** Implementing robust, privacy-focused data storage solutions.

### 3. Technical Delivery: Why Web/PWA?

- **Ubiquity:** Frictionless access across all platforms.
- **Low Entry Barrier:** No installation requirements (Zero-install).
- **Dynamic Deployment:** Enables near-instant updates and CI/CD capability.
- **Cost Efficiency:** Significantly lower development and maintenance overhead compared to native mobile apps.
- **Broad Accessibility:** Democratizing content distribution.

---

## Phase 2: Prototyping

- Development of a Minimum Viable Product (MVP) to validate core features and user experience.

---

## Phase 3: Development Methodology

Selection of a strategic development paradigm:

- **Iterative:** Focusing on repeating cycles of development to refine the product based on feedback.
- **Incremental:** Focusing on delivering functional components of the product in segments.
